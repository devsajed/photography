WHAT DRIVES ME

My Philosophy

My photography is a reflection, which comes to life in action and reaction. You will see in my work: both creative and documentary photography. My focus is always on the story. I am a storyteller. I am a photographer.

To me, photography is about making sense of the world. Photography is about finding appreciation in the small things in life. Photography is about walking slower, looking at things, and finding beauty in the ordinary and mundane. Photography is a meditation on life and death.
